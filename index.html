Raquel Breternitz is an award-winning designer, speaker, and writer. She is passionate about inclusion, accessibility, and keeping tech human (and human-focused).

Right now, she works as a Lead Product Designer at the New York Times. Prior to now, she worked as the Design Director for Elizabeth Warren for President. She speaks on accessibility, inclusion, design, and politics.
